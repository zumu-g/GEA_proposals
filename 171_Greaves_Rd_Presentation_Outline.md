## DESIGN DIRECTION

### Visual Style
- **Palette:** Warm neutrals, deep greens (reflecting gardens), charcoal accents
- **Typography:** Serif headlines (premium feel), clean sans-serif body
- **Imagery:** Full-bleed hero images, ample white space, no clutter
- **Layout:** Asymmetric, editorial magazine feel

### Image Requirements
- Hero property shots (twilight preferred)
- Garden/landscape details
- Pool and entertaining areas
- Shed/workshop feature
- Lifestyle stock imagery (premium quality only)
- Aerial/drone perspective

### Tone of Voice
- Confident, not arrogant
- Knowledgeable, not condescending
- Exclusive, not elitist
- Personal, not corporate
