**Slide 11: Stage 1 — Discreet VIP Campaign**
*NSFTD Principle: Scarcity + Exclusivity*

Duration: 2 weeks (prior to public launch)

Strategy:
> Over two weeks, we launch a carefully curated off-market campaign aimed at a select group of VIP buyers. This positions the property as exclusive and rare — available only to discerning clientele before the wider market.

Tactics:
- **Personal outreach** to our database of qualified premium buyers
- **Targeted invitations** to business owners, executives, and affluent families
- **Private evening showings** — by appointment only
- **Gourmet catering** and professional staging create a memorable experience
- **Buyer's agent network** alerted to the opportunity
- **Teaser campaign** on social media (no address) builds intrigue

Objective:
> Create urgency and secure offers before public launch — or build a shortlist of serious buyers ready to compete.

*Visual: Elegant invitation mockup / evening showing imagery*
